MUTUAL NON‑DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Mutual Non‑Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of July 1, 2025, by and between AlphaTech Solutions ("Party A") and Beta Innovations ("Party B").

1. Definition of Confidential Information. Includes business plans, financials, customer lists, trade secrets, and technical data shared during discussions.

2. Obligations. Each party shall not disclose Confidential Information or use it beyond evaluation of the potential relationship.

3. Term. The obligations continue for 3 years from the date of disclosure.

4. Exclusions. Information that is public, lawfully received from third party, or independently developed is excluded.

5. Required Disclosures. Permissible disclosure under law only after prior written notice to the other party.
